ABOUT THE FAIR

Intended to bridge the gap between student interns and the startups, the Placement Cell of Jamia Millia Islamia will be hosting a one day Internship Fair. The fair will bring on board 40 startups seeking interns in various fields, giving them an option to select from a pool of talented students. The fair will be a perfect platform for students to meet potential employers, create networks and gain challenging opportunities to work for this summer.
